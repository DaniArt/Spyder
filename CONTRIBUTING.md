# Contributing to Spyder

Thank you for contributing to Spyder.

- Your contribution is your original work.
- You grant the Spyder project a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive license to use, modify, and distribute your contribution under the Apache 2.0 license.

If you do not agree to these terms, please do not submit contributions.
